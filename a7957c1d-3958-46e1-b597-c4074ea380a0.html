GET DETAILED FEEDBACK AND STATS

Identify your weaknesses and eliminate them. Aim Lab tasks measure your performance by skill and provide detailed analytics and insights on how to improve. Your Aim lab data profile shows your performance across tasks and skills to help you measure your progress over time.


CHOOSE YOUR GAME SETTINGS AND GO!

Pick the game settings that you want to use, check your graphics settings and go! You can input the sensitivity for the game you play without needing to mess around with converters. Just select the game title from our list of over 500 FPS & TPS titles.

GO BEYOND AIM TRAINING

Great FPS players have fundamental skills beyond aim. You will need to train your visual detection speed, attention capacity, short term memory, audio-spatial skills, and decision-making. Aim Lab can help you train all of the skills required to become a legend.






CUSTOMIZE YOUR ROUTINE

Customize every detail of your training, from weapons, to crosshair settings, and tasks. Train with your controller! Coming soon: Create your own maps, bots and tasks from scratch in the workshop.
