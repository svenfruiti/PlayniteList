About Touhou:Shooting Star -Touhou Ryuseitan


As a wizard "Marisa Kirisame", Fly in the Gensokyo!
This is fun game of "Touhou Project"!
Let's play "Danmaku"!

３D Danmaku Battle

Fly and fight in the Gensokyo.
3-directyional bullets is reaching to you.
Avoid bullets and shot down enemys!

Spell Card

Boss enemys use "Spell Card".
Defeat their beautiful danmaku and Get ther Spell Card!
Analyze and avoid characteristical bullet patten.

Develop your weapon

Get materials from defeated enemys and develop more strong weapon, armor, and spell card.
Make your own equipments set.
Each weapon has parameters and bullet.
Each Armor has parameters and skills and appearance.

Quest

This game includes more 30 quests!
Enemy boss character is more than 10 !
And every quest has sub targets.
Let's try "No damage clear","Time attack","Get all spell cards" and so on.

Talk in Gensokyo

There are many characters of "Touhou Project" in the village and can talk to them.
Let's talk as Marisa!
There is original illust of characters of Touhou Project.

Boutique

You can change Marisa's cloth.
Find your favorite cloth and fly in Gensokyo.
